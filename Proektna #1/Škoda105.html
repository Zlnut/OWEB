Škoda 105

The Škoda 105 is a compact family car produce by the Czechoslovakian manufacturer Škoda Auto between 1976 and 1990. It was part of the Škoda 742 series, which included it's larger siblings, the Škoda 120 and the Škoda 130. The Škoda 105 was an evolution of the earlier Škoda 100/110 series and featured a more modern design with a rear-engine, rear-wheel-drive layout. Today, the Škoda 105 is considered a classic and is sometimes seen at vintage car rallies, appreciated for its retro charm and historical significance as part of Eastern European automotive history.
