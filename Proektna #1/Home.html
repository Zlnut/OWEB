Used

Many of these models we bought from people getting sick of Yugo-Nostalga or eldery citizens looking to upgrade to a robust german car. In any case almost all of these models were in rough shape. We took them to our garage and serviced them until they were better than any previous moment in their lifetime. Why not celebrate their new life by taking them out for a drive?
